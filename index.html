Oh no! You and your friend have been tasked to help Professor Oak with his research, but don't know his password. Luckily, the professor has left clues to figure it out in your respective labs. Can you solve the pokemon-themed puzzles before the non-existent timer runs out? Play remotely or in the same room with a friend (or by yourself with two screens I won't judge)

Tips

  1. The game works best on laptop, I've been too scared to try it on mobile so do it at your own risk
  2. Taking notes is very helpful, but try not to show your partner your notes
  3. Unless you have encyclopedic knowledge about the entire Pokemon series, you WILL need to look things up
  4. Remember that your partner cannot see your screen. Most clues will require "something" from both of you, so be sure to describe everything

How to Play

  1. The game works best on laptop, I've been too scared to try it on mobile so do it at your own risk
  2. Decide who will be player 1 and who will be player 2
  3. Click on the respective buttons to start
  4. You have escaped when both players show "You escaped" on their screen
